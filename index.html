Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RV storage

Our facilities can accommodate RVs of all sizes, from small camper vans (under 20 feet) to large Class A motorhomes (up to 45 feet). Each location has different size limitations, so please check the specific facility details or contact us for oversized RV storage options.

We offer flexible billing options. You can pay monthly, quarterly, or annually. Payments are automatically charged to your credit card on file. We accept all major credit cards and also offer ACH bank transfers. You'll receive email receipts for all payments, and can view your payment history in your account dashboard.

All our facilities feature 24/7 video surveillance, perimeter fencing, coded gate access, and well-lit premises. Many locations also have on-site managers and regular security patrols. We take the security of your RV seriously and continuously invest in upgrading our security systems.

Most facilities offer 24/7 access with your personalized gate code. Some locations may have restricted hours for indoor storage units. Always check with your specific facility for their access hours. For after-hours access needs, many locations can arrange special access with advance notice.

We offer flexible cancellation with 30 days notice for monthly contracts. There are no long-term commitments - you can cancel anytime after your initial term with proper notice. For prepaid annual contracts, we offer prorated refunds. Please review your specific contract terms or contact our customer service for details.
